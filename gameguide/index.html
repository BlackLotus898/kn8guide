Banner Types
  • THE GAME Gacha — The permanent banner. Always available and features the standard pool of characters and weapons. Use Dimensional Crystals or THE GAME Gacha Tickets.
  • Pickup Gacha — Limited-time banners featuring a specific rate-up ★5 character and their signature weapon at increased odds. These are the best opportunities to pull for specific units. Use Pickup Gacha Tickets or Dimensional Crystals.
Pity System
  • Every pull earns you Gacha Medals. You can exchange 250 medals for a ★5 character and 150 medals for a ★5 weapon — this is the pity system.
  • If 9 pulls in a row all result in weapons, your next pull is guaranteed to be a ★4 or higher character. This applies across single pulls and 10-pulls, and even across different Pickup Gachas.
  • The total ★5 drop rate is 8% — 3% for characters and 5% for weapons. The pickup rate for the featured character is 1%.
  • Pickup Gacha Medals do not expire — exchange them at your own pace. 250 for a ★5 character, 150 for a ★5 weapon.
  • Rate-up banners have increased odds for the featured character, making them the best time to pull for specific units.
How to Earn Dimensional Crystals
  • Story Modes — Main Story, Anime Story, and Memory Stories all reward Crystals on first clear.
  • Daily & Weekly Missions — Consistent daily play provides a steady stream of free Crystals.
  • Events — Kaiju Rush, Fierce Battle Area, and limited-time events are among the best free sources.
  • Player Rank — Each rank-up grants a Crystal bonus.
  • First-time clears — New content and stages always come with one-time Crystal rewards.
Always use the Web Shop: The official web store at webstore.kj8-thegame.com gives significantly more Dimensional Crystals per dollar than purchasing directly in-game. Never buy Crystals through the in-game shop if you can avoid it.
Smart Pulling Tips
  • Prioritize pulling on limited banners featuring characters that fill a gap in your squad composition.
  • Don't impulse pull on every banner — limited characters return in reruns eventually.
  • Save at least 100+ pulls before targeting a limited banner to have a reasonable chance at the rate-up unit.
  • Check a character's page before pulling to confirm they fit your playstyle and squad.
📖 Story Modes // Narrative Content ▼

K8 The Game features three distinct story types, each with unique rewards and lore. Completing them all is one of the best sources of free Dimensional Crystals early on.

Main Story

The primary campaign featuring original CLOZER characters and a brand-new narrative set after the events of the main series. A mysterious Dimensional Gate spawns Kaiju previously thought neutralized.

Unlocked from the start. Complete this first for the bulk of early Crystal rewards.

UNLOCKED AT START

Anime Story

Relive the events of the Kaiju No. 8 manga and anime with full voice acting from the original cast. A faithful retelling with game-exclusive cutscenes and illustrations.

Great for fans of the source material and newcomers alike. Awards Crystals on first clear of each stage.

UNLOCKED AT START

Memory Stories

Character-focused episodes for ★5 officers. Each tells a personal story for a specific character. Owning the featured character unlocks a special Home Screen background.

Awards Dimensional Crystals and character upgrade materials. Some of the best content in the game for lore fans.

UNLOCKED AT RANK 6
Priority: Rush all three story modes as soon as they're available. The first-clear Crystal rewards are among the largest single sources of free currency in the game, especially for new accounts.
🏆 Events // Limited Content ▼

Events are time-limited content that rotate regularly. They provide some of the best rewards in the game including Dimensional Crystals, upgrade materials, and exclusive items. Never skip active events.

Kaiju Rush

A multi-stage survival event where your squad fights through consecutive Kaiju encounters. HP carries between fights so resource management is key. Build strength and endurance to clear further stages.

Dimensional Crystals Upgrade Materials Kaiju Core Materials

Raids

Community cooperative battles against a specific Kaiju boss. You receive 3 permits per day to challenge the boss and deal as much damage as possible. The event runs until the community defeats the boss together.

Dimensional Crystals Upgrade Materials

Fierce Battle Area

A limited-time challenge mode with unique stage effects each run. Try to clear the highest stage possible — rewards scale with how far you climb. Read the stage effects before entering and build your squad around them.

Dimensional Crystals Emblem of Defense Proof of Valor

Mop-Up Operation

Triggered after a Raid boss is defeated by the community. Farm Reinforcement Materials and Unimaterials. Neutralize as many Kaiju as possible in a single battle for better rewards — rare 5-Star Unimaterials can drop.

Reinforcement Materials Unimaterials 5★ Unimaterials (Rare)

Large Conquest

Form two squads to fight endless waves of Kaiju. Neutralize as many as you can to reach reward milestones. AoE characters like Rin, Soshiro, and Kafka are especially useful here.

Dimensional Crystals Emblem of Defense Proof of Valor

Story-driven limited events tied to specific characters or narrative arcs. These often introduce new characters on rate-up banners and include exclusive story content alongside mission rewards.

🏅 Exploit Medals // Character Equipment ▼

Exploit Medals are special equipment tied to specific Kaiju. Each character is designed to use a particular medal for maximum effectiveness. The medal provides a unique bonus when equipped on the matching character.
