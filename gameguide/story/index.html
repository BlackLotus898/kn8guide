📖 Story Modes // Narrative Content ▼

K8 The Game features three distinct story types, each with unique rewards and lore. Completing them all is one of the best sources of free Dimensional Crystals early on.

Main Story

The primary campaign featuring original CLOZER characters and a brand-new narrative. A mysterious Dimensional Gate spawns Kaiju previously thought neutralized.

Unlocked from the start. Complete this first for the bulk of early Crystal rewards.

UNLOCKED AT START

Anime Story

Relive the events of the Kaiju No. 8 manga and anime with full voice acting from the original cast.

UNLOCKED AT START

Memory Stories

Character-focused episodes for ★5 officers. Owning the featured character unlocks a special Home Screen background.

UNLOCKED AT RANK 6
Priority: Rush all three story modes as soon as they are available. First-clear Crystal rewards are among the largest single sources of free currency in the game.
