🏆 What Are Achievements? // Overview ▼

Achievements are one-time goals that reward Dimensional Crystals for completing specific conditions. Some also unlock Special Requests — additional missions with large Crystal payouts. They unlock after clearing Main Story Chapter 0-7.

  • How to access: Home → Menu (top right) → Achievements
  • How to claim: Tap any box with a yellow dot in the top right corner to collect your reward.
  • Some achievements have hidden conditions — the unlock requirement is only revealed after you complete it.
  • Some achievements unlock Special Requests when completed. A "Special Request Unlocked" pop-up appears at the top of the screen when this happens.
  • Always read Special Request messages to the end — both the unlock message and the post-clear message each reward Dimensional Crystals ×20.
📋 Known Achievements // How to Complete ▼
Achievement How to Complete Rewards
Fighting Alone Clear any Training stage with only Kafka Hibino (The Man Who Became a Kaiju) in your squad. Use the lowest difficulty Training stage. The 5-star Kaiju No. 8 unit does NOT count — must be Kafka. Crystals
With My Own Strength Clear 10 battles with Kafka Hibino alive and without him transforming (don't use his Ultimate). Any stage counts including Training. Disable Ultimate in AUTO settings to be safe. Crystals
This Is Who I Am Right Now Defeat Myxogasterocarp using Mina Ashiro's Ultimate. Recommended stage: Memories 2-10, where a Lv.40 guest Mina is available. Weaken the boss with Normal Attacks first, then finish with her Ultimate. Crystals Special Request
Same Aspirations Clear Training: Honju Neutralization Difficulty 2 or higher with Kafka Hibino and Mina Ashiro as Battle Buddies. Requires Spec-Ops Rank 2 to unlock Honju Neutralization. They just need to be in the squad — defeat doesn't matter as long as you clear the stage. Crystals Special Request — Strength (×200 + ×20)
Efficient Neutralization Also obtained in Memories 2-10 using the same strategy as "This Is Who I Am Right Now." Completing both in one run is possible. Crystals
The Clean-Cut and the Wild Child Clear Training: Attribute Material Difficulty 5 or higher with Haruichi Izumo and Aoi Kaguragi as Battle Buddies (1 time). Crystals
Family Restaurant Discussion Raise Kafka Hibino, Kikoru Shinomiya, and Reno Ichikawa to Level 40. Crystals
I'm Saving You Clear Training: Honju Neutralization Difficulty 3 or higher with Reno Ichikawa and Iharu Furuhashi as Battle Buddies (1 time). Requires Spec-Ops Rank 2. Crystals
Nobody Gets to Quit Clear a battle without the squad taking any damage while Kikoru Shinomiya is in the squad. Crystals
Slice Open a Path Clear Training: Role Material Difficulty 3 or higher with only 1 Soshiro Hoshina in the squad (1 time). Crystals
Note: Some achievements still have unknown conditions — they are revealed only after completing them. More will be added as they are discovered.
