🏆 Events // Limited Content ▼

Events are time-limited content that rotate regularly. They provide some of the best rewards in the game. Never skip active events.

Kaiju Rush

A multi-stage survival event where your squad fights through consecutive Kaiju encounters. HP carries between fights so resource management is key.

Dimensional CrystalsUpgrade Materials

Raids

Community cooperative battles against a specific Kaiju boss. You receive 3 permits per day. The event runs until the community defeats the boss together.

Dimensional CrystalsUpgrade Materials

Fierce Battle Area

A limited-time challenge mode with unique stage effects each run. Rewards scale with how far you climb. Read the stage effects before entering.

Dimensional CrystalsProof of ValorEmblem of Defense

Mop-Up Operation

Triggered after a Raid boss is defeated by the community. Farm Reinforcement Materials and Unimaterials. Rare 5-Star Unimaterials can drop.

Reinforcement MaterialsUnimaterials

Large Conquest

Form two squads to fight endless waves of Kaiju. Neutralize as many as you can to reach reward milestones.

Dimensional CrystalsProof of Valor

Chapter Events

Story-driven limited events tied to specific characters or narrative arcs. Often introduce new characters on rate-up alongside exclusive story content.

Dimensional CrystalsCharacter Materials

Mini Special Events

Smaller limited-time events that run between major updates. Quick to complete but worth doing for Crystal and material rewards.

Dimensional CrystalsMaterials
