📊 Progression Overview // Where to Start ▼

Characters in K8 The Game are strengthened through several systems. Focus on your core squad first before spreading resources thin.

1
Level Up — Use EXP materials to raise character level. Each level increases base stats.
2
Level Limit Break — Hit the cap? Break it using Honju Neutralization materials and the appropriate Spec-Ops Rank.
3
Skill Enhancement — Level up each of the character's 5 skill types using attribute and role materials plus Exploit Medals and Kaiju Core Materials at higher levels.
4
Ascension (A1–A6) — Unlock powerful passive bonuses using Talent Shards earned from duplicate pulls.
5
Equip & Level Weapon — Equip the best weapon and level it up using weapon EXP materials. Ascend it up to 4 times using duplicate weapons.
6
Develop Uniparts — Craft and equip the recommended Unipart set using Unimaterials from Training.
Priority order: Level → Skill Enhancement → Ascension → Weapon → Uniparts. Skills provide the biggest power jump per resource spent.
⬆️ Leveling & Level Limit Break // Raising the Cap ▼

Characters have a level cap that increases with your Spec-Ops Rank. To keep leveling past the cap you need to perform a Level Limit Break using materials from Training: Honju Neutralization.

How to Farm Leveling Materials
  • Training: Basic Material — Primary source of EXP materials (Kaijumite). Run this whenever you have stamina to spare.
  • Training: Honju Neutralization — Drops Level Limit Break materials for both characters and weapons. Essential for breaking the level cap.
  • Higher Spec-Ops Rank unlocks harder Training stages with better material yields.
Tip: Don't save EXP materials — use them immediately on your active squad. Stamina is always regenerating so materials will keep coming.
✨ Skill Enhancement // Biggest Power Spike ▼

Each character has 5 skill types that can be leveled up independently. Fully upgraded skills can be nearly twice as effective as base level. Always check a character's Skill Upgrade Priority on their page before spending materials.

Materials Required

Role Materials

Attacker, Supporter, or Defender enhancement materials based on the character's role.

Source: Training → Role Material

Attribute Materials

Slash, Shot, Blunt, Fire, Wind, Ice, or Lightning materials matching the character's attack type.

Source: Training → Attribute Material

Exploit Medals

Required for mid-to-high level skill upgrades. Each character uses a specific medal type.

Source: Training → Identified Kaiju Neutralization (5×/week, 0 stamina)

Kaiju Core Material

Required for the final levels of skill enhancement. ★5 characters need it from Skill Level 9+. Very rare.

Source: Emblem of Defense Shop, Encounter Stone Shop, Defense Force Pass, Events

Don't spread thin: Leveling all skills on every character wastes materials. Focus on the top 2–3 skills of your main squad first, following their Skill Upgrade Priority.
🔺 Ascension (A1–A6) // Passive Upgrades ▼

Ascension unlocks powerful passive bonuses for each character across 6 nodes (A1–A6). Each node requires Talent Shards, which are earned from pulling duplicate copies of that character.

General Priority
  • A1 and A2 first — These typically provide the strongest direct power increases and are the highest priority.
  • A3 and A5 are Skill Level nodes — They raise the level cap of specific skills. Only unlock these when you have the materials to immediately use the extra levels — otherwise the node does nothing.
  • A4 and A6 — Vary by character. Check the individual character page for their specific Ascension Priority rating.
Note: Each character has unique Ascension effects. Always check the Ascension Priority on a character's page before spending Talent Shards.
💥 Unlocked Potential // Post-Max Enhancement ▼

Unlocked Potential is an advanced enhancement system available for select fully maxed characters. It allows further skill and stat upgrades beyond normal limits using Potential Shard materials.

Currently Available For
  • Isao Shinomiya (The Unbreakable Iron Wall)
  • Mina Ashiro (Off the Scale)
  • Soshiro Hoshina (Hoshina-Style Sword-Slay Technique)
Materials Required
  • Stages 1–4: Potential Shard I ×250 each
  • Stage 5: Potential Shard II ×100
How to Get Potential Shards
  • Potential Shard I — Training: Unlock Potential Material (1×/day, 0 stamina, unlocks at Spec-Ops Rank 2)
  • Potential Shard II — Event rewards only
Requirement: Reach Spec-Ops Rank 2 or higher to access Unlocked Potential. Full character max is not required.
⚔️ Weapon Leveling & Ascension // Gear Upgrades ▼

Weapons can be leveled up and ascended, similar to characters. Always level and ascend the weapon on your main Attacker first.

Weapon Leveling
  • Level weapons using weapon EXP materials from Training: Basic Material and Honju Neutralization.
  • Weapons have level caps tied to your Spec-Ops Rank, just like characters.
  • Unlike characters, leveling weapons has a smaller impact — prioritize characters first.
Weapon Ascension
  • Weapons can be ascended up to 4 times (A1–A4) using duplicate copies of the same weapon.
  • Ascension enhances the weapon's effect (passive ability) — it does not increase base stats.
  • Weapon Ascension does not cost Credits — always ascend weapons whenever you have duplicates. There is no reason to hold back.
  • Check weapon effects before ascending to understand what you're improving.
Disassemble wisely: Disassembling a ★5 weapon gives 1 Refinement Crystal for the Weapon Exchange Shop. Don't disassemble unless you have a duplicate or truly don't need it.
