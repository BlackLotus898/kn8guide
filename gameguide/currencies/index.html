💵 Credits // General Currency ▼

Credits are the general-purpose currency used for leveling characters and weapons, performing Level Limit Breaks, and upgrading skills. Always keep a healthy stock or you won't be able to progress with Reinforcements.

How to Earn
  • Training: Basic Material tab — The best Credits-per-stamina source. Run this regularly.
  • Other Training tabs — Role Material, Honju Neutralization, Attribute Material, and Identified Kaiju Neutralization also drop smaller amounts of Credits alongside their main rewards. Note that the Unimaterial tab does NOT include Credits.
  • Player Rank ups — Each rank-up rewards Credits automatically.
  • Events & community milestones — The developers occasionally gift Credits alongside other rewards.
Tip: If you have excess Credits, spend them in the Shop to buy Reinforcement Materials — don't let them sit idle.
💎 Dimensional Crystals // Premium Currency ▼

Dimensional Crystals are the primary gacha currency used to pull on all banners. They can be earned for free or purchased.

Free Sources
  • Daily Missions — Up to 50 Dimensional Crystals per day from completing daily tasks.
  • Story Mode — Each combat stage awards Crystals on first clear. Non-combat stages also give Crystals. Push story as far as possible early.
  • Player Rank ups — Each rank-up includes a Crystal bonus.
  • Events — Kaiju Rush, Fierce Battle Area, Raids, and limited events are among the best free Crystal sources.
  • Special Requests — Unlock at Player Rank 10. Each request and its follow-up message rewards Crystals.
  • Promotion Exams — ×20 Crystals from the unlock message and ×20 from the post-clear message for each exam.
  • Achievements — Various in-game achievements reward Crystals.
  • Serial Codes — Redeem codes from official livestreams and events at the Web Store.
Web Store: Always buy Dimensional Crystals from webstore.kj8-thegame.com instead of in-app — you get significantly more Crystals for the same price.
🛡️ Emblem of Defense // FBA & Large Conquest ▼

Emblems of Defense are earned from Star Rewards in the Fierce Battle Area and Large Conquest. They are exchanged in the Shop for Kaiju Core Materials and other upgrade items.

How to Earn
  • Fierce Battle Area — Earn Emblems of Defense from Star Rewards by clearing stages. The higher the stage, the more you earn.
  • Large Conquest — Also awards Emblems of Defense from Stage Star Rewards.
Exchange Priority
  • Kaiju Core Materials — Top priority. These are rare and essential for character upgrades.
  • Other upgrade materials — Spend remaining Emblems on materials you need most.
🔮 Refinement Crystals // Weapon Exchange ▼

Refinement Crystals are earned by dismantling ★5 weapons and are used to exchange for ★5 weapons in the Weapon Exchange Shop.

How to Earn
  • Dismantle ★5 weapons — You receive 1 Refinement Crystal per ★5 weapon dismantled. Go to Home → Storage to dismantle manually.
How to Use
  • Exchange for ★5 weapons in the Weapon Exchange tab of the Shop. Limited to 1 exchange per month, resetting on the 1st at 04:00 JST.
  • Best exchange: GS-3305 if you have Gen Narumi — his best-in-slot weapon. Otherwise exchange for any ★5 weapon you don't own yet that suits your squad.
Note: Don't dismantle ★5 weapons unless you have duplicates or truly don't need them — Refinement Crystals are hard to come by.
🦎 Kaiju Core Material // Rare Upgrade Material ▼

Kaiju Core Material is one of the rarest upgrade materials in the game. It is required in the final stages of character Skill Enhancement — ★5 characters begin consuming it from Skill Level 9. Collect it whenever possible.

How to Earn
  • Emblem of Defense Shop — Exchange Emblems of Defense in the Shop monthly. This is the most reliable source. Don't miss it each month.
  • Encounter Stone Shop — Available with a monthly exchange limit. Priority should go to Gacha Tickets first, but grab these if you have leftover Stones.
  • Defense Force Pass — The premium season pass includes Kaiju Core Materials as rewards. One of the main reasons to consider purchasing the pass.
  • Kaiju Rush — Mission completion rewards include Kaiju Core Materials when the event is active.
  • Limited-time events — Special Events and Mini Special Events occasionally include Kaiju Core Materials in their reward pools.
Priority: Always buy Kaiju Core Materials from the Emblem of Defense Shop first — it's the best free source. Then check the Encounter Stone Shop after grabbing your Gacha Tickets.
