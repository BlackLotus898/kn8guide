What is K8 The Game?

A free-to-play Kaiju-slaying battle RPG developed by Akatsuki Games, based on the hit Shonen Jump+ manga and anime. Released September 30, 2025 on Steam, iOS, and Android.

⚔️
Turn-Based Combat

Select officer skills to attack. Expose the Kaiju's core, then unleash devastating Ultimate attacks.

👥
Squad of 4

Assemble a four-person squad from the Japan Anti-Kaiju Defense Force. Synergy matters.

📖
Three Story Modes

Original narrative, anime/manga retelling, and character-focused Memory Stories.

💎
Gacha System

Pull for ★5 characters using Dimensional Crystals. Get more from the Web Shop than in-game.

🏟️
Combat Zone

15-level monthly endgame mode that resets each month. Build strength to climb further.

🌐
Regular Events

Kaiju Rush, Fierce Battle Area, and limited-time banners keep the content cycle fresh.
