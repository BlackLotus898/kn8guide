Ailments & Special Status

Hydro-Load

A stackable resource unique to this unit. Max 10 stacks. Consumed by Ultimate — each stack consumed increases that attack's DMG Multiplier by 7.3%.

Torrent

Applied to all allies after Mina activates Ultimate (A6). After an ally attacks with Combat Skill, randomly destroys 1 of the target's PLT and removes Torrent from that ally.
