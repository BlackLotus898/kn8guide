// Status Effects

Special Status

Prestige

All allies' CRIT DMG +16%. This unit's Normal Attack becomes Enhanced (Thunder-Flicker Slash). Greatly increases chance of this unit being targeted by Kaiju. Duration decreases by 1 after this unit's own turn, not at the end of the round.

Sass

Stacks up to 3. Gained after Enhanced Normal Attack or Ultimate. At max stacks: consumed entirely to grant Evasion Rate +100% (1 turn) and Additional DMG Multiplier +30% (2 turns) to self, and Additional DMG CRIT Rate +16.5% (2 turns) to all allies. With A6, also grants DMG dealt +80% and Additional DMG Multiplier +100% (3 turns) to self.
