// Status Effects

Special Status

Fury

ATK +16.5% and Shot DMG dealt +22% (+30% additional with Hard-Won Pride at Lv.60). After Combat Skill, randomly deals Shot Additional DMG (up to 5 times) and gains 1 stack of Refinement (max 2). After Ultimate, consumes 1 Refinement stack. At max Refinement stacks, all stacks are consumed, Fury ends, and Pride begins. Entered at battle start (with Innate Drive) and before each Ultimate use.

Pride

Recovers 1 SP after each Combat Skill use. Removed after Ultimate. Entered when Refinement reaches max stacks while in Fury.

Refinement

Stacks up to 2 (max). Gained 1 stack per Combat Skill use while in Fury. Consumed 1 stack per Ultimate use. At max stacks, all are consumed and Fury transitions into Pride.
