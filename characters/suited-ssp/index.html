Passive Skill

Swimsuit Power

  • Each time allies recover a total of 300 HP, applies 1 stack of Radiance to this unit. Max 50 stacks.
  • After turn arrives, based on Radiance stack count, activates the following effects:

    • [45 or more stacks]: Consumes 30 stacks of Radiance and applies DMG dealt +9.9% (2 turns) to all allies.

// Status Effects

Ailments & Special Status

Hydration

Increases the target's Max HP. Applied to all allies after activating Ultimate or at battle start (A6). Max HP Boost +13.7% per application.

Radiance

A stackable resource unique to this unit. Max 50 stacks. Generated each time allies recover 300 HP total. Triggers passive effects at 25 and 45 stacks.

Refreshing Rain

Applied to all allies by Combat Skill. After the target receives an attack, recovers their HP equal to 6.4% of their Max HP (max 1 activation), then removes itself.
