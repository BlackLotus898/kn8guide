// Status Effects

Ailments & Special Status

Poison

Inflicts DoT based on max HP at the start of the action. Maximum damage depends on the inflictor's ATK.

Stun

Prevents the target from acting for a certain number of turns. Increases CRIT DMG taken when hit.

Stealth

Will not be targeted by Kaiju attacks. Becomes a selectable target if all allies are in Stealth state.
